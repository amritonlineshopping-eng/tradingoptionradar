Trading Setups Used in This System
Smart Money Concepts (SMC)
What it does: Identifies institutional support/resistance zones where big money accumulates or distributes.
Why it works: Institutions control 60%+ of market flow. Their order blocks create natural reversal zones.
When to take it: When price bounces OFF an order block (buy at support OB, sell at resistance OB).
ICT Fair Value Gap (FVG)
What it does: Spots price gaps left unfilled — zones where price moved too fast, leaving imbalance.
Why it works: Markets naturally return to fill gaps, creating predictable entry points with defined risk.
When to take it: When price pulls back into an unfilled gap and shows rejection candle.
Opening Range Breakout (ORB)
What it does: Trades breakouts from the first 15-minute candle range with PCR and VWAP confirmation.
Why it works: The first 15 minutes set the day's directional bias. Breaks of this range attract momentum.
When to take it: After 9:30 AM when price breaks ORB high (CE) or ORB low (PE) with volume surge.
Kill Zone (Opening & Power Hour)
What it does: Exploits the highest-probability windows when institutional momentum is strongest.
Why it works: Algorithmic order flow and institutional activity spikes at market open and during power hour.
When to take it: 9:15–10:30 AM (opening) and 1:30–2:30 PM (power hour).
Price Action & Candlestick Patterns
What it does: Reads wick rejection, engulfing and pin bar patterns to spot high-probability reversals.
Why it works: Candles reveal where buyers and sellers fought for control. Large wicks = rejection = reversal.
When to take it: After a confirmed rejection candle closes near a key support or resistance level.
Open Interest (OI) Buildup
What it does: Detects when institutional money enters (Long Buildup) or exits (Unwinding) the market.
Why it works: OI + price direction = institutional conviction. Buildup = trend continues. Unwinding = reversal.
When to take it: CE on Long Buildup (price up + OI up). PE on Short Buildup (price down + OI up).
